# Teachmo Glossary

## A
**Activity:** A discrete learning task or event assigned to a student, either by a teacher or a parent.
**Audit Log:** An immutable record of security-critical actions (login, data export, permission change).

## D
**Deep Link:** A URL that navigates directly to a specific resource (e.g., a specific assignment) rather than the dashboard.
**Digest:** A summary of notifications sent via email to reduce noise.

## M
**Mitigation:** A temporary rule applied by the Orchestrator to suppress notifications during a "storm" (e.g., stopping 50 messages in 1 minute).

## O
**Orchestrator:** The central logic engine that decides *when* and *how* to send notifications based on user load and priority.

## P
**Pod:** A small group of families (opt-in) within a class for community support.

## R
**Roster:** The list of students, teachers, and their relationships, usually synced from an SIS (Student Information System).

## S
**SIS (Student Information System):** The system of record for the school (e.g., PowerSchool, Skyward).
**Snapshot:** A point-in-time record of metrics used for fast dashboard loading.

## T
**Tenant:** An isolated environment for a specific school district. Data never crosses tenant boundaries.

## W
**Weekly Brief:** An AI-generated summary of a student's progress, upcoming events, and focus areas, sent to parents every Sunday.
